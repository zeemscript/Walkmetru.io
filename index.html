Frequently Asked Questions

Can I earn by sharing what I know on Walkmetru?

Yes! Generate extra income by sharing your skills globally. Create a free profile, set your availability, and let others book one-on-one video sessions at rates you determine. Additionally, you can offer courses for personalized 1-to-1 learning directly from your profile.

Can I learn and consult on walkmetru?

Certainly! Learn and consult by booking 1-to-1 sessions with skilled professionals. Search for specific skills, book their calendar, or join 1-to-1 classes and sessions. Walkmetru offers a versatile learning and consulting platform for everyone

What level of experience is required to share knowledge on Walkmetru?

Whether you’re an expert or a beginner, you can share your knowledge and experience on Walkmetru through live 1-to-1 calls. The key is to teach something you could share with a friend for free. Your audience and fees depend on your expertise level, making it accessible for everyone, regardless of experience. Beginners may cater to those with little to no experience, offering a valuable learning experience at a competitive rate.
